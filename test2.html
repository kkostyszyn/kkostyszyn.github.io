Current events

I'm a teaching assistant for LIN200 (Language in the USA), and am helping prepare for the second round of SYCCL (Summer Youth Camp for Computational Linguistics) through Stony Brook University's IACS.

Additionally, I am lab manager for Arthur Samuel's speech perception lab.

Personal Info

I graduated from Bryn Mawr College in 2017, spent a year doing research in Irene Vogel's Prosodic Typology lab at the University of Delaware, and am currently a PhD student at Stony Brook University.

My research interests include computational phonology, morphophonology, phonotactics, and modeling language learning.
